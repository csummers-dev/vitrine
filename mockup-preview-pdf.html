Q1 2024 · CONFIDENTIAL

Quarterly performance overview

The first quarter of 2024 saw filebrowser pretty deployments grow 38% quarter-over-quarter, driven primarily by homelab adoption among the self-hosting community. New feature uptake tracked above projections: the Stage 8 inline workflows replaced 73% of legacy modal interactions within four weeks of release, exceeding the 50% adoption threshold set in our quarterly OKRs.

Highlights

Dark-mode adoption exceeded expectations at 64% of active users opting in within the first sprint of availability. The keyboard-shortcut overlay introduced in Stage 11g saw 12% weekly-active engagement, suggesting strong demand for power-user affordances.

Risks & mitigations

Zip-extraction (Stage 12) ships behind a default-on flag with operator-level disable via CLI. We're tracking the zip-bomb defense thresholds across the first cohort to calibrate the 1% compression-ratio floor for real-world archives. Initial telemetry suggests the threshold is well tuned — zero false positives across 8,400 legitimate extractions sampled.
